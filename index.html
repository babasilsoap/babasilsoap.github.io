品嘉的一天

白天

這是品嘉的一天，早上睡到9:00。「挖這是個美好的清晨，我要去學校了」品嘉說。到了學校後已經快到吃飯時間了，準備來去買麥當勞，一份大麥克、一份大薯、一份麥克雞塊跟大悲可樂。吃到一半睡著了，先休息吧。起床後已經三點了，繼續吃著沒吃完的大鼠。

傍晚

差不多可以下班了，品嘉說。看看時間現在下午四點零五分。整理整理就騎著車回家了。

晚間

品嘉晚上回家太累了，一直睡到凌晨一點起床。

半夜

品嘉一點起床了，一路玩到早上八點，睡了一小時就到學校了…

結論

就這樣，日復一日，年復一年。

Exp.1 Simple transmittance

Principle：

In a transmission experiment, a beam of light is directed onto a material sample, and the remaining Intensity of the light after passing through the material is measured. By measuring the transmission of the light, the absorption and transmission properties of the material for light of different wavelengths or energies can be obtained.

For GaSe material, when the energy of the photon is higher than 2.0 eV, it excites electrons from the valence band to the conduction band in the material, causing the light to be absorbed by the material and unable to penetrate through it. When the energy of the photon is lower than 2.0 eV, the photon energy is insufficient to excite electrons in the material, so the light can penetrate through the material.

Equipment：

  1. The sample of GaSe
  2. Micrometer screw
  3. Tape (stick GaSe on it)
  4. Detector
  5. Fiber
  6. Computer
  7. Lamp
  8. Lens

Sample preparation：

Exfoliate layered crystal until thin layer with blue tape. ( For GaSe, the thickness is around 0.0025 cm.) Stick the thin crystal on the tape and start the experiment.

Procedure：

  1. Turn off the light and erase the light signal (Push the dark lamp icon, then push the erase dark lamp icon).
  2. Prepare the GaSe sample and necessary equipment for the experiment.
  3. Use blue tape to peel off a thin layer of material and stick it onto a transparent tape.
  4. Align the tape with the sample onto the fiber hole and stick it.
  5. Turn on the light source and use a lens to focus the light onto the sample(the fiber hole).
  6. Turn on the detector, computer, and program, and start the measurement.
  7. Pause the screen and save the data as a txt file.
  8. Plot the graph using Origin program and examine the experimental results.
